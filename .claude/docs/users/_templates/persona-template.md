## Usage Instructions

**Critical Rule**: Personas describe **people and their problems**, NOT **features and solutions**.

### Anti-Patterns (Solution Space)
- "Needs dashboard with analytics" → "Needs to see if efforts are working"
- "Needs bulk upload feature" → "Needs to submit many items without repetition"
- "Needs gamification with badges" → "Needs recognition for contributions"

**Avoid**: Specific features, UI/UX solutions, technical implementations, "needs [feature name]"
**Focus**: Underlying problems, what they're trying to accomplish, context and constraints
